The Current Challenges with Ad Evaluation

Brands invest highly in terms of time and resources to test their concepts and creatives before launching their ad campaigns. While concept and creative testing provides a level of assurance to the marketers, there are many shortcomings to the current methods of testing that include surveys and focus groups.

User: Growth Marketer, Performance Marketer, Creative Director.

Challenges:
  • Failing to pinpoint why behind success of failure of campaign
  • Limited sample size in surveys and focus groups
  • Intensive investment of time and cost
  • Subjective judgments and uninformed opinions of people
  • Ad blindness
  • Lack of context
  • Controlled environment which doesn't reflect the real world
  • Scalability of current ad testing methods
  • Bias, comparison, and information processing fatigue
